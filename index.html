Accessibility Checklist

Review this checklist to ensure the profile page follows accessibility best practices.

  1. Use landmark elements such as <header>, <main>, and <footer>.
  2. Provide meaningful alternative text for images.
  3. Use headings in a logical order, starting with one <h1>.
  4. Ensure link text describes the destination or action.
  5. Keep color contrast high for readability.
  6. Support keyboard navigation with visible focus states.
  7. Use lists for related items and instructions.
